A (special) currency converter

The currency converter was one of the first exercises I had to perform in PHP during my web development training. Here, I've decided to create it in JavaScript, but without using an API. I'm using a fictitious currency: the Simoleon (§)! After a relentless investigation full of pitfalls, I've found an exchange rate that's more or less reliable (if you ignore the paradoxes that don't always reflect the prices of our world, such as a 40§ pizza opposed to a beautiful 4,000§ brand new car).
